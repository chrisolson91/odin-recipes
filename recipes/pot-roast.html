Mississippi Pot Roast

Healthy Version of the Clasic Mississippi Pot Roast

A healthier version of the classic spicy, flavorful beef dish made with au jus & ranch seasonings, butter and pepperocinis! This Paleo Mississippi Pot Roast is made with real ingredients and itâ€™s gluten free, dairy free, and Paleo.

Ingredients

  • 3 lb Chuck Roast
  • 1 tsp Better Than Bouillon Beef Base
  • 1 tbsp Coconut Aminos
  • 2 cloves Minced Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Parsley
  • 1 tsp Chives
  • 1 tsp Dill
  • 2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Onion Flakes
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 1/2 tsp Sea Salt
  • 3 tbsp Butter
  • 8 Pepperocinis
  • 20 Baby Carrots
  • 8 Gold Potatoes
  • 4 stalks Celery

Steps

  1. Place chuck roast in slow cooker. Surround chuck roast with potatoes, baby carrots, and celery
  2. Bring 1/2 cup water to a boil in microwave and combine 1 tsp of Better Than Bouillon beef base
  3. Add coconut aminos and minced garlic to the beef broth
  4. Mix dried seasonings together in separate bowl (skip this step if using pre-mixed seasonings)
  5. Pour beef broth mixture over top of chuck roast and sprinkle all seasonings (3 tbsp of pre-mixed seasonings) evenly on top of everything
  6. Place the butter on top of the meat in 3 spoonfuls. Place the pepperocinis on and around the chuck roast
  7. Cook on low for 8-10 hours (or high for 4-5 hours)
